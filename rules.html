Rules of the Quiz

  1. You will have only 15 seconds to answer all question.
  2. You can only select one [1] answer per question.
  3. Once you select an answer move on to the other question.
  4. Once you hit the Continue button you will need to hit Start Quiz for the timer to start
  5. You can't Exit or Go Back while taking the Quiz
  6. You'll get points base on your correct answer.
  7. If you finish before time is up you will need to click Submit to check your answers
  8. You will get a pop up stating your score then you will directed to the final page.
  9. You may retake the quiz from this point or you may exit the quiz
